Habilis

The email-to-Dropbox gateway

Here at Goo Towers we really love iPads and we really love Dropbox.com, so it niggled us that we couldn't create content on our iPads and upload it straight to Dropbox. We don't like being niggled, so we went ahead and invented Habilis, the email-to-Dropbox gateway.

Using Habilis is really simple. Visit gethabilis.com, use the single-click sign-up and you'll get your own gethabilis.com email address. Email files to that address and they'll appear in your Dropbox.com dropbox. We just couldn't make it any easier!
